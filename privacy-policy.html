Your privacy is important to us, and maintaining your trust and confidence is one of our highest priority. To protect your personal information from unauthorized access and use, we use security measures that comply with federal law. We respect your right to keep your personal information confidential and understand your desire to avoid unwanted solicitations.

We are committed to protecting your customer information in every transaction, at every level of our organization. We are committed to helping you protect your privacy every day.

Personal Information we Collect

This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the HFC Website.

* HFC may collect nonpublic personal information to complete the request (i.e. if applied for a new loan) by client.

* We safeguard your customer information carefully.

Use of your Personal Information

  • HFC collects and uses personal information to deliver the services you have requested.
  • We collect and maintain customer information as part of servicing your account and your customer relationship.
  • We maintain policies and procedures covering the proper physical security of workplaces and records.
  • HFC may also use your information to inform you about the updates of HFC or the updated mortgage rates.

  • HFC will disclose your personal information as otherwise permitted or If Government entities, courts or other entities (in response to subpoenas and other legal processes), or those with whom you have requested us to share information.
